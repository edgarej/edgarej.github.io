Edgar E.J.'s Portfolio Website

Hi, welcome to my portfolio website. I am an artist who specializes in narrative illustration and character creation. I have a background in illustrating environments for interior design projects. I started out my career in the education industry working in various teaching positions. Currently I work as a freelance artist as well as a generalist for Cookies by Design. To see my full CV, check out my LinkedIn link in my socials menu. Also check out a portion of my art portfolio below and on my art profile links. Read my thoughts and fiction in my journal link in the navigation menu, and watch my art tutorial videos on my YouTube. Thank you for visiting, and have a great day.
