About Me

Hi, thank you for visiting my portfolio website. I am an artist who specializes in narrative illustration and character creation. I have a background in illustrating environments for interior design projects. I started out my career in the education industry working in various teaching positions. Currently I work as a freelance artist as well as a generalist for Cookies by Design. To see my full CV, check out my LinkedIn link in my socials menu. Also check out a portion of my art portfolio down below and on my art profile links. Read my thoughts and fiction in the journal link above, and watch my art tutorial videos on my YouTube.
